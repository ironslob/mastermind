Mastermind

How to play

Crack the hidden code in 20 guesses. Everyone gets the same puzzle each day.

Build a row of 4 colours from the palette below. Tap a colour to fill slots left to right. Tap a filled peg to remove it.

After each guess you receive feedback:

  • Correct colour, correct position
  • Correct colour, wrong position

Colours can repeat. Good luck!
